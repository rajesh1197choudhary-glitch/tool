Life Expectancy & Wellness Checker

This free tool gives you a fun life expectancy estimate based on lifestyle habits. It’s only for educational and entertainment purposes—not medical advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this life expectancy tool?

This tool uses general averages and lifestyle habits to give a fun estimate. It is not medical advice.

Is my data safe?

Yes, all calculations happen in your browser. No information is stored or sent to servers.

How can I improve my life expectancy?

Stop smoking, eat a balanced diet, exercise regularly, sleep well, manage stress, and always use safety gear.
